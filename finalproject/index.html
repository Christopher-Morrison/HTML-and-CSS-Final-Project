Rome

Rome was overwhelmingly large and packed full of wonderful sights. Surprisingly, the people were very polite to tourists, which is not the case in all Italian cities. I have visited Rome twice because I loved it so much. There truly is an unlimited number of things to see. Every church was extraordinary, regardless of its size. Rome had a way of showing you your place in the universe. Walking into a building that is over 2000 years old and significantly more beautiful than anything else you've ever seen just leaves you in awe.
